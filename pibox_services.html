and why

Benchmarks from our active M710q node in France. Data cross-referenced with sharedata.app. Updated March 2026.

PiBox V1 default stack — 4 apps Selected for maximum earnings with zero resource conflict. Each app does a different job: no bandwidth splitting, no IP flagging risk. Based on monthly benchmarks and sharedata.app community data.
Why only 4 apps? Running 6–8 bandwidth-sharing apps simultaneously fragments your available bandwidth between them — each app earns less than it would alone. More apps also increases the risk of IP flagging by your ISP, and account suspensions from apps that detect "commercial" patterns. 4 complementary apps is the optimal balance between earnings and safety.
