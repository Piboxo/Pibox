⚠️ Honeygain — why it's not in the main stack

Honeygain has aggressive account suspension policies for residential IPs in France. Their ToS interpretation around "commercial" bandwidth use has led to bans for several users. We replaced it with PacketStream (more stable, more tolerant). Honeygain is still available as an optional install but is not recommended as a primary app.
