Why 4 apps and not 8?
These 4 apps do different jobs: Salad and earn.app share bandwidth, MystNodes routes VPN traffic, bitping probes latency. No two apps compete for the same resource. Running 6–8 bandwidth-sharing apps simultaneously splits your available bandwidth between them, reducing earnings per app — and risks IP flagging by your ISP or account suspension. 4 is the sweet spot.
